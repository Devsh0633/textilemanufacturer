Crafted Collections,
Rooted in Tradition

Every piece is hand-printed in Jaipur using traditional wooden blocks, shaped by time, skill, and human touch.

How to Experience the Collection

Our fabrics are not manufactured; they are created. Each yard bears the unique impression of the artisan's hand. Slight variations in color and print are not flaws—they are the hallmark of authenticity.

“What you see is not a flaw — it is a signature of the hand.”

Handblock Printed Fabrics

Our core collection features premium cottons, silks, and linen blends, each printed with natural and azo-free dyes. From intricate floral 'bootis' to geometric 'jaals', our designs traverse centuries of Rajasthani heritage.

Ideal for designers, boutiques, and those who value authenticity.
