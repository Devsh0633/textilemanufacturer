A Heritage Woven Through Generations

Heritage Village Textile was born from a deep respect for India’s handblock printing tradition — a craft shaped by time, patience, and human touch.

Rooted in Jaipur’s Timeless Craft

Jaipur is not just a city; it is the heartbeat of handblock printing. For centuries, the rhythmic sound of wooden blocks striking fabric has echoed through its streets. Heritage Village began as a humble attempt to preserve this melody.

Founded by a family of master printers, our factory was built on a simple promise: to keep the art alive in its purest form. In a world racing towards automation, we stood still, honoring the hands that carve, dye, and print with a precision no machine can replicate.

We are a community of artisans, designers, and dreamers, united by the belief that true luxury lies in the imperfections of the handmade.

More Than Fabric — A Responsibility

We believe that when something is made by hand, it carries the soul of its maker. Our philosophy is simple: culture over trends, skill over shortcuts, and time over speed.

The Art of Handblock Printing

It starts with a block of teak wood and a steady hand. A design is carved, millimeter by millimeter, a process that can take days. Then comes the color—nature's own palette of indigo, turmeric, and madder root.

The printing itself is a dance. The artisan aligns the block, strikes it firmly, and lifts it to reveal the impression. This is repeated hundreds of times across a single saree or bedspread. Finally, the fabric is washed and sun-dried, absorbing the warmth of the Rajasthan desert.

Crafted with Care — For People & Planet

Natural Dyes

We harness the power of plants and minerals to create colors that are safe for the earth and your skin.

Minimal Machinery

Our factory runs on human energy. We keep our carbon footprint low by relying on traditional hand tools.

Low Waste

We recycle our water and repurpose fabric scraps, ensuring that nothing goes to waste in our cycle of creation.

Artisan Livelihoods

We are committed to fair wages and dignified working conditions, ensuring our artisans thrive alongside the craft.

From Jaipur to the World

While our roots are deeply embedded in the sands of Rajasthan, our branches reach across the globe. We collaborate with designers, boutiques, and textile lovers from Paris to New York, bridging the gap between ancient tradition and modern aesthetics.

We see ourselves as cultural ambassadors, sharing the beauty of Indian craftsmanship with a world that is rediscovering the value of the handmade.
